Differential-abundance analysis across **phosphoproteomics, proteomics, and
metabolomics** datasets. R workflows: differential expression and volcano plots,
**KSEA** kinase-substrate enrichment (kinase-score heatmaps), **harmonizR** batch
harmonization with random-forest modeling, and **MSEA / QEA / GSEA** against
MSigDB and Reactome gene sets.
